------------ Problems ----------------

fuel gauge GPOUT has a battery level pullup. This required a trace cut because it should be pulled up to 3.3V. Add back in with a voltage divider.

CHG status is not connected to an RTC pin, so the device can't be automatically woken up by USB connection.
USBdetect pin cannot be used instead because it does not pull low.

board capacitance takes over 10 seconds to bring 3.3v rail to 0v. This causes the display to artifact when powering down. 
Might need a mosfet to completely power down the display, or some way to discharge the leftover power
