Biggest issues:

1. When the power gets too low, The the battery I'm using doesn't protect itself and can get undervolted, killing the battery.
I have not yet managed to successfully enter a dead battery state where the firmware detects the battery is low. It works fine at higher voltages,
but when the battery actually gets really low (like 3.4 volts), I run into big issues.

2. I have multiple times got the device into an unexplainable state when switching batteries. The battery voltage rails read about 2.2V on a brand new battery,
even though the battery is closer to 4V when disconnected from the device. The device will not boot properly. After disconnecting and reconnecting the battery multiple
times, it will suddenly start behaving normally. This is obviouslly a huge problem that would need to be solved to make this device worth giving to other people
